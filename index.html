What's The Deal With
Lootboxes?

Why Buy Them?

Due to both the social stigma of not having the best cosmetics or not owning the best piece of gear in the game, users succumb to their urges and purchase these items with real-life money.

When the user obtains an extremely rare item from a loot box, they receive a dopamine rush making them want to continue purchasing them.

Why Are Lootboxes Bad?

Games that have a pay-to-win aspect attached to loot boxes also become difficult for consumers who do not purchase loot boxes as they cannot beat the pay-to-win players with their gear, forcing them to either purchase loot boxes or continue to lose.

These companies know exactly what they are doing and understand that they can profit greatly if consumers continue to purchase these loot boxes and accept the status quo.

Definition

Loot boxes are consumables that a user can open up and they are then given a prize ranging anywhere from a cosmetic item to items that provide game changing effects.

Certain video games, such as CS:GO, allow players to use their skins (which have a real-life value) in online gambling sites, which are no different than the types of games found in casinos.
